Aesthetics of Windots

Frutiger Aero
A glossy, optimistic UI style from the mid-2000s. It uses glass-like surfaces, soft lighting, gradients, and natural imagery (like sky, water, grass). It feels “alive” and polished, like the interface is floating in space rather than sitting on a screen.
Y2K
A chaotic early-internet aesthetic with chrome textures, bright colors, and experimental UI design. Everything feels bold, slightly messy, and futuristic in a retro way — like people were guessing what the future would look like.
Clean Core
A modern minimalist design style. Flat UI, sharp edges, simple layouts, and focus on usability. It removes decoration and prioritizes structure, speed, and clarity over visual complexity.
